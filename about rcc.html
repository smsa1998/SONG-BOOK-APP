ABOUT REVIVAL CENTERS CHURCH-THE HOLY SPIRIT EXPERIENCE

REVIVAL CENTRES CHURCH HISTORY

Our foundation is based on the events recorded in Acts 2 in the Bible, where the
disciples of Jesus were filled with the Holy Spirit. This same experience, with the
Bible sign of speaking in other tongues, is still how people join the church today.

"......and they were all filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak
with other tongues, as the Spirit gave them utterance – Acts 2:4"

Our founding pastor, the late Lloyd Longfield, was filled with the Holy Spirit in 1948.
From these exciting beginnings, Revival Centres has grown into a world-wide
fellowship of spirit-filled believers. Over the years we’ve been known as
Revival Centres of Australia, Revival Centres International, and more recently
Revival Centres Church. Importantly, an adherence to the Full Gospel message hasn’t changed!

THE HOLY SPIRIT EXPERIENCE

Having a personal relationship with God is the promise of the bible.
In a Revival Centre you will meet normal people who have had an extraordinary experience.
The new testament of the bible describes a church that changed the world, full of people who knew God.
It talks about salvation as a foundational experience from an answering God.

"Do you want to receive the Holy Spirit NOW?"

Jesus didn’t die to bring us dead religion. He came to fulfil God’s plan, and pour
out the Promise. He didn’t rely on finding strong, smart, good people.
Instead He sent the Holy Spirit to change people. He called it being born again.
Afterwards, nothing would be the same again. Ever.

ABOUT YOUNG PEOPLE

The Revival Centre youth group runs from early high school age, through to young adults.
Youngies is a great opportunity to spend time with Spirit-filled people, who have the same Holy Spirit experience.

The Youngies run great activities most weekends. These activities focus on good,
clean fun and encouragement from each other, and the Bible. Most Revival Centre locations have a Youngies group.
If you are interested in joining in, please feel free to contact us for more details!
